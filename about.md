When I was about four years old, I wanted to grow up and get paid to take photos of animals while traveling the world. That didn't happen.   

In 2020, I graduated from the University of Florida with a Bachelor of Science in Statistics. I took courses in machine learning, probability, and computer science. That's pretty close, right? 

Currently, I am a data analyst at a nonprofit focused on protecting democracy. I'd like to improve the way law interacts with technology and statistics. I'm inspired by W.E.B. Du Bois, who [visualized](https://www.smithsonianmag.com/history/first-time-together-and-color-book-displays-web-du-bois-visionary-infographics-180970826/) 
the meaningful connection between policy, history, and data long before `ggplot2`.
